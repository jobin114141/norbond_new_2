OUR ENTITIES

At Norbond, our global reach is supported by a network of diverse entities, each designed to enhance our operational efficiency and serve as the backbone of our multifaceted business.

NORBOND

NORBOND ENTERPRISES

Norbond Enterprises is the core entity that oversees the management and strategic direction of all our brands. With a presence in key regions such as the UAE, UK, and India, Norbond Enterprises acts as the parent company, coordinating the operations and growth strategies of all affiliated brands and subsidiaries.

Dubai, UAE | London, UK | Kochi, India

Operations Headquartered in Kochi, India, Norbond Enterprises serves as the central hub for business development, innovation, and market expansion across Asia, Europe, and the Middle East.

CORPORATE COTTON

Specializing in the textile and fabric industry, Corporate Cotton is one of the key entities within the Norbond portfolio. Known for its sustainable and high-quality cotton products, Corporate Cotton provides the raw materials and fabric solutions used in several of our home and lifestyle brands.

Dubai, UAE

Based in Dubai, UAE, Corporate Cotton focuses on manufacturing and sourcing premium cotton fabrics, offering solutions for both local and international markets.

LUXE DECORA INC LTD

Luxe Decora INC Ltd is the official corporate entity behind the Luxe Decora brand, a leader in premium home décor and lifestyle products. This entity handles everything from design and production to distribution and marketing of Luxe Decora's curated collections.

London, UK

Located in London, UK, Luxe Decora INC Ltd ensures that the brand's luxurious vision and dedication to quality is carried out in all regions, blending British craftsmanship with international expertise.

Jumbble

JUMBBLE GIFTS TRADING CO LLC

Jumbble Gifts Trading Co LLC is the entity responsible for managing the Jumbble brand, specializing in premium bean bags and seating solutions. This entity coordinates the design, manufacturing, and distribution of Jumbble's wide range of products.

Dubai, UAE

Headquartered in Dubai, UAE, Jumbble Gifts Trading Co LLC oversees operations across the Middle East and other global markets, ensuring each bean bag design upholds the brand's commitment to quality and comfort.
